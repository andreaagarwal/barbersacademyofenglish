About Your Tutor

English isn't just a language. It's a way of carrying yourself.

Eddie Barber was educated at Repton, one of England's oldest and finest public schools, where proper English — spoken, written, and lived — was simply the air one breathed. Six decades later, it still is.

CELTA-certified and endlessly patient, Eddie teaches the English that opens doors: the confident introduction, the well-turned phrase, the right word at the right dinner party. His students — professionals, families, and ambitious learners from around the world — come for the grammar and stay for the charm.

Away from the lesson table, Eddie is a qualified personal trainer with a keen belief that a sharp mind deserves a healthy body — and he brings a coach's discipline and encouragement to every lesson. Progress, properly paced.

"Anyone can teach you English. I'd rather teach you to be at home in it."

Questions, answered properly

The things prospective students most often ask.

What is Barbers Academy of English?
Barbers Academy of English is a private English tuition service based in London, England. It offers one-to-one British English lessons online worldwide, small group conversation classes, and in-person lessons in London. All lessons are taught by founder Eddie Barber, a CELTA-certified English teacher educated at Repton School.
Who is Eddie Barber?
Eddie Barber is the founder and principal tutor of Barbers Academy of English. He is a CELTA-certified English teacher, educated at Repton School, one of England's oldest public schools. He teaches British English, pronunciation, business English and British etiquette to adult professionals, families and international students from London and around the world. He is also a qualified personal trainer, and brings a coach's discipline and encouragement to language learning.
Are lessons available online?
Yes. Private one-to-one lessons and small group conversation classes are taught online by video call, with students in all time zones including China, Hong Kong, the UAE and Saudi Arabia. In-person lessons are also available in central London.
What is the CELTA qualification?
CELTA is the Certificate in Teaching English to Speakers of Other Languages, awarded by Cambridge. It is one of the most widely recognised English teaching qualifications in the world, and it means Eddie Barber is a formally trained and assessed English teacher, not simply a native speaker.
Do you teach British etiquette as well as English?
Yes. British etiquette, manners and social confidence are woven into lessons alongside the language itself — introductions, small talk, dining etiquette and the courtesies of English social and business life. In-person London lessons can include practical etiquette such as afternoon tea.
Who are the lessons for?
Lessons suit adult professionals polishing their business English, families relocating to or visiting the United Kingdom, and any learner who wants to speak proper British English with confidence. Many students come from China, Hong Kong and the Gulf. All levels from intermediate upwards are welcome.
How much do English lessons cost?
Fees depend on the lesson format — private one-to-one, small group, or in-person in London — and how often you wish to study. Send an enquiry through the website or email eddie@barbersacademyofenglish.com and Eddie will reply personally with a recommendation and fees, usually within a day.
How do I book a lesson with Barbers Academy of English?
Send an enquiry through the contact form at barbersacademyofenglish.com or email eddie@barbersacademyofenglish.com. Eddie replies personally, usually within a day, and arranges a relaxed introductory conversation — online or in London — before lessons begin. There is no test and no obligation.
